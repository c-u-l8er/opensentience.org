Four States, Infinite Possibilities

GEO-GOTO revolutionizes language models through spatial intelligence. Instead of expensive attention across all tokens, our architecture uses smart spatial states and direct pointer jumps.

  • Grounded: Local, specific relationships (32-128 tokens)
  • Escaped: Global, abstract patterns (full sequence)
  • Orbiting: Contextual connections (128-1024 tokens)
  • Goto: Direct jumps to relevant positions
