Geospatial Intelligence Made Simple

OpenSentience combines advanced geospatial processing with an intuitive Domain Specific Language (DSL) to enable rapid development of location-aware applications without deep GIS knowledge.

  • Geospatial DSL: Macro-based syntax compiling to efficient Tile38 commands
  • Real-Time Streaming: Sub-second location updates with Phoenix PubSub
  • Intelligent Geofencing: Time-based rules and dynamic zone creation
  • Route Optimization: Smart dispatching with real-time adaptation
  • Analytics Engine: Transform location data into actionable business intelligence
