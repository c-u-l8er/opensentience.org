v0.5 · Addendum Seven economic invariants are added across five existing groups, each marked with a small accent dot in the cell corner. They are not a separate ledger; they are claims that the existing substrate — κ routing, σ resolution, β persistence — is already an economy, and that a healthy agent ecosystem needs more than one currency to express what it produces. The argument follows Lietaer, Ulanowicz & Goerner (2010): complex flow networks survive only inside a window of vitality balancing efficiency against resilience, and a monoculture of a single token pushes any such network past the brittleness threshold. Empirical anchor: the Swiss WIR (1934–present, Stodder 2009), eighty years of countercyclical evidence for multi-currency stability.
